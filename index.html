PRIVACY POLICY

What Accord stores about you

Accord is a thin convenience layer over Google Forms. We don't sit between you and the form data — Google does. The minimum information we keep is what's needed to remember who you are between visits and to autofill the fields you configure.

What we collect

  • Google account identity — your display name, email, and avatar URL, exactly as Google's OAuth returns them. Used to verify you and to prefill form fields.
  • Profile rules — the field rules you configure (label, match type, patterns, value). Stored under your user ID in our database.
  • Accords you create — the form URL, slug, entry IDs, and prefill values you save. Linked to your Google account.

What we don't collect

  • We do not see, store, or log the responses you submit to Google Forms. Submissions go directly from your browser to Google.
  • We do not run analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party advertising scripts.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Third parties

Accord uses Google Sign-In for authentication and Google Cloud Firestore for data storage. Both are operated by Google under their own privacy terms.

Deleting your data

Delete an Accord or a profile field from your dashboard at any time and it's removed from our database immediately. To remove your account entirely, open the user menu in the top-right of the dashboard and choose Delete account — your profile, every Accord you've created, and your sign-in are wiped right then.
